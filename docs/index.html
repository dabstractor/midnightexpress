Serving the Denver, Sherrills Ford, and Lake Norman Region

Midnight Express Lkn is a family-owned and operated airport shuttle service based in the Sherrills Ford and Denver, NC surrounding areas. We specialize in reliable, pre-booked flat rate transportation to and from Charlotte Douglas International Airport, Concord Regional Airport, and nearby private airfields.

Our licensed and insured drivers are known for being punctual, professional, and courteous, and we always use clean, well-maintained minivans. Our drivers track your incoming flight for your convenience so we're always on time to pick you up. We never charge extra for anything. We always offer true flat rate pricing: no surprise up-charges for extra bags or delays.
